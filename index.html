White Paper

Executive Summary

Metalink Social is a decentralized, NFT-driven social platform where users control their digital identity, privacy, and content. Our universal blockchain code links all social presences while protecting user data, promoting ownership and innovation in the digital age.

Introduction

Web2 social networks limit user privacy and monetize content without fair rewards. Metalink Social’s Web3-first design enables verifiable ownership, cross-platform connection, and a new model for digital social freedom.

The Problem

Centralized platforms hold all user data and restrict identity transferability, stifling innovation, privacy, and fair monetization. True ownership remains out of reach for most users today.

Vision & Mission

To empower every person with a secure digital identity, under user control, unlocking Web3 monetization, privacy, and true cross-network connection.

Core Technology

Every user receives a unique NFT-based code stored on blockchain—linking and verifying all connected platforms, with cryptographic proof of identity, and protected by decentralized storage and end-to-end encryption.

Key Features

  • Universal, blockchain-backed user code
  • User-owned, portable digital identity
  • Decentralized privacy and access control
  • Monetization for creators and users
  • Interoperability across Web3 & social networks

Roadmap

2025 - Beta launch
2026 - NFT identity rollout
2027 - Universal code social integrations, monetization for creators, DAO governance.

Team

Our diverse team brings expertise in blockchain, cybersecurity, social UX, and digital rights—committed to building user-first platforms for the future.

Conclusion

Metalink Social is more than another network—it is the foundation for the next phase of social connection, privacy, and ownership.
